About Us

Mission

    The Doane Dumangas Fundamental Christian Academy(DDFCA)is a bible-centered school committed to provide quality education for pre-school and elementary children. Aims to provide a child centered learning environment contributing to the development and competence of a person for a service to God and Humanity.

Vision

    To be a model of excellence in Christian education by transforming the hearts and minds through quality education demonstrating the life and teaching of Jesus Christ.

Core Values

                                       Maka-Diyos (Faith of God),                                    Maka-Tao (Love of Humanity),

                                       Maka-Kalikasan (Love of Nature),                          Maka-Bansa (Love of Country)
